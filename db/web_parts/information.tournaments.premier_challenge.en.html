Premier Challenge

The 5th annual Premier Challenge will be held August 17th and 18th.  We will host up to 96 teams in age groups U9, U10 and U11; in Gold/Silver I and Silver II/Bronze level brackets.  As an early season tournament its a good tournament for newly formed teams, testing their pre-season form, or returning teams whom are stepping up to gold level competition.  Most games are played on well-maintained grass fields, good playing conditions for the warm summer months.
