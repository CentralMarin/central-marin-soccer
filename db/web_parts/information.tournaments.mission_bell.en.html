Mission Bell

The Mission Bell Classic was inaugurated in 1997 in memory of Claire Ann O'Neil, a well-respected member of the Marin Soccer Community.  The 16th annual Mission Bell Tournament, will be held the second weekend in August; 10th and 11th.  This year we will host up to 96 teams in age groups u-12, U-13, U14, U15/16 (combined) in Silver/Bronze/Division III levels.  As an early season tournament, the Mission Bell is good pre-season preparation, a testing ground for newly formed teams, as well as those returning teams, who are playing at the highest level of competition. Games are played on both turf and well-manicured grass fields.
